Welcome to Adul-ting!!!

The inspiration for this website stems from the questions we have all asked ourselves: "Why don't they teach us something useful in school? Like how to get a mortgage." We aim to inform young adults on topics (such as tax; credit score; mortages, NI and much more) to help prepare them for life beyond education.
